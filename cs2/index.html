Mobile & Web Technologies
Taught on behalf of Cardiff Metropolitan University at 西安科技大学高新学院.
Objectives

The goals of this course are to study popular, modern tools, languages, frameworks, and libraries for developing front end, UI experiences as both websites and mobile apps. We will also study networking and architecture concepts that make large scale web apps a possibility.
